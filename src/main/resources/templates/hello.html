About This App

1. Select a Political Cause

Choose a cause that you are passionate about. This provides the core context for our AI to generate a focused and relevant appeal that speaks directly to the issue.

2. Find Your Senators

We need to know who your representatives are to address the email correctly. Search by your postcode or select your state to find your current Senators.

Senators

Select which Senator you would like to contact. Each email we generate is uniquely tailored to the specific background and party of your chosen representative.

Providing these details helps our AI craft a more authentic and empassioned appeal that truly reflects your unique voice. The more context you give us, the better we can link your personal experience to the cause and make a compelling case to your Senator.
